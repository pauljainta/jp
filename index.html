Introduction

Hello there! I'm Jainta Paul, a tech enthusiast born and raised in Tangail, Bangladesh. I graduated in Computer Science from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). After graduation, I embarked on an exciting professional voyage, spending 18 months honing my skills at OpenRefactory, Inc where I worked with code analysis techniques. Now, I find myself immersed in the stimulating academic environment of the University of Utah, pursuing a Ph.D. under the expert guidance of Dr. Luis Garcia. My passion lies in safeguarding the Security, Safety, and Privacy of Cyber-Physical Systems, a field I believe holds the key to shaping our digital future. Beyond my academic pursuits, I am blessed to share my life's journey with the love of my life, adding joy and fulfillment to every step I take. I am also a fan of football.
