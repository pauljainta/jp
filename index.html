About

Hello there! I'm Jainta Paul(First Name Pronounced as: Joy-On-To. I know it's difficult. In that case call me JP), born and raised in Tangail, Bangladesh. I graduated in Computer Science from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). After graduation, I worked for 18 months at OpenRefactory, Inc as a Software Engineer where I worked with static code analysis techniques. Now, I find myself immersed in the stimulating academic environment of the University of Utah, pursuing a Ph.D. under the expert guidance of Dr. Luis Garcia. My current research focuses on the Security, Safety, and Privacy of Cyber-Physical Systems. I am blessed to share my life's journey with the love of my life, adding joy and fulfillment to every step I take. I am also a fan of football and follow FC Barcelona and Argentina National Team. And yes, Leo Messi is the GOAT!
